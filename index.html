Frequently Asked

How quickly can I launch?

From signed retainer to first delivery is typically twelve weeks — one month design, one month sampling, one month print, dye and finalising. Production runs 8 – 12 weeks for standard CMT or 6 – 8 weeks via our RTL catalogue.

What is the MOQ?

Production from 30 units per style, per colour. Founders typically test a tight first range and grow into volume as the line proves itself.

What is included in the monthly retainer?

The full pre-production journey: design, sample-room, sourcing, pattern drafting, techpack, toile and true sample, plus production quote, atelier booking and pre-shipment inspection. Production costs themselves sit on a separate order docket once samples are approved.

What sits outside the retainer?

Bulk production costs (CMT, fabric, trims, packaging), extra sampling beyond your tier's allowance, swatches and strike-off samples beyond inclusions, custom artwork beyond scope, and freight. Everything is quoted transparently before commitment.

How does the lock-in work?

Three-month minimum on every tier. After the minimum, one month's written notice to cancel. No lock-in beyond the minimum. You can change tiers at any monthly cycle.

Can I work with EDC from outside Bali?

Yes — most founders do. Onboarding, fit sessions and reviews happen by video, all artefacts live in your Drive, and design leads are reachable on WhatsApp in Studio and Maison tiers. Studio visits are welcomed by appointment.

Are your suppliers actually certified?

Yes. Our sourcing network is anchored to Lenzing™, OEKO-TEX®, ECONYL®, Tencel™ and REPREVE® mills, with GOTS-aligned cotton and recycled-wool partners. Documentation is kept on file and shared with Maison clients in their annual sustainability impact report.

What if my toile isn't what I expected?

Designs may shift in sampling due to fabric, finish or material variation. The first revision works from fit images; the second from physical sample. Additional sampling rounds are quoted clearly — Studio and Maison tiers include more upfront.
